Vastu Rules Dictionary

Core principles for a harmonious home

Main Entrance Vastu

The main door is the archway to prosperity. It should ideally face North, East, or North-East. Avoid South-West entrances as they are associated with instability. Ensure the door is well-lit and larger than other doors in the house.

Kitchen Layout Rules

Fire rules the South-East corner (Agneya). This is the absolute best placement for a kitchen. The cook should face East while preparing meals. Never place the kitchen directly under or above a pooja room or bathroom.

Master Bedroom Direction

The Earth element dominates the South-West, bringing grounding and stability. The master bedroom must be located here. Sleep with your head pointing South or East for deep, restorative sleep. Avoid mirrors facing the bed.

Pooja Room Guidelines

Jupiter rules the North-East (Ishan corner), making it the spiritual center of the home. Place your Pooja room here. Ensure the space remains clutter-free to allow divine energies to circulate. Face East or North while praying.
